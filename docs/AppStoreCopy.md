## Description

Next Caltrain shows exactly when the next train leaves.
No more digging through timetables or squinting at tiny rows of numbers.

LIVE COUNTDOWN
See a big, clear countdown to the next train, updating every second.
At a glance, know if you need to run or if have time to grab a coffee.

KNOWS THE SCHEDULE, SO YOU DON'T HAVE TO
Caltrain runs different schedules on weekdays, weekends, and holidays.
Next Caltrain automatically detects which schedule applies today — and even
shows you tomorrow's first trains late at night, so you're never caught off guard.

CLEAN, FOCUSED DESIGN
No clutter from skipped stations, no dense spreadsheet-style tables. Just
your route, your next train, and the full stop-by-stop schedule when you
want it — all in large, easy-to-read type.

WORKS OFFLINE
Schedule data is cached on your device, so you're covered even with no
signal underground or between stations.

Next Caltrain is built for daily riders along the Peninsula — Local,
Limited, Express, and South County (Gilroy) service all included.
